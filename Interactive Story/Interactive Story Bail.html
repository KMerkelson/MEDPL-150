If any of them could afford that expenditure, they’d snap up Count Addesso’s offer themselves. You don’t begrudge their professional ruthlessness—you’d do the same thing.

So if your fellow potionmakers can’t be counted on to help, and you’ve rejected the mob, how will you solve this problem on your own?
