You charge like a madman! The two of you topple to the ground with crash. You punch at the uncovered parts of his face, but he’s strong enough to hold you back. As soon as he’s recovered from the shock, he pushes you off of him. As the two of you rise, his hand reaches for his club.

But by the time he brandishes his club, you’ve already splashed the acid in his face.

The next day, the papers report on the robbery. A fortune in siren scales stolen—and a young man brutally killed. The polizia say they are hard at work looking for the perpetrator, and considering the merchant’s deep pockets, they probably are.

But you don’t have time to worry about them knocking on your door. You have to finish brewing this potion with hands that won’t stop shaking.
