DEAL WITH THE DEVIL

When all else fails, The Syndicate is known to offer assistance to those in need, as long as those poor souls have something useful to offer them. But whatever price they’ll ask of you will be one that is within your means to pay…if not one that you can pay without regret.

Through some carefully worded wheedling to a vendor everyone knows (but doesn’t know) is in their pocket, you make it clear that you’re interested in cutting a deal. Not long after you return home, a bag is thrown over your head and you’re roughly carried away to their base of operations.

When you can see again, you’re in a dark room. A weaselly man in a fine, purple-lined overcoat sits on the other side of a mahogany desk. Next to him stands a hooded woman holding the unmistakable athame of a witch—a mage of ill-repute, specializing in vicious curses and magically-enforced contracts. After some perfunctory politeness, the gangster slides one such contract across the desk to you.

They are willing to offer you the siren scales—enough to brew the Draught of Correspondence, with a little extra in case of error. In exchange, they want you to brew them a large batch of Elixir of Cloaking…or in layman’s terms, invisibility potion. You can’t help but wonder what they need this for...

Especially because there are persistent rumors that the Syndicate wants to rob your guild.

Are you willing to take the deal, knowing that risk?
