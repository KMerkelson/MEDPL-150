BAD END 2

You work nonstop trying to fix the potion. You pour your blood, sweat, and tears into it (but not literally, that would be gross). You’ll swear to your dying day that you tried your goddamn hardest.

But it wasn’t enough. When Count Adesso comes to collect his due, he leaves disappointed.

You are left to sit in the mess of your workshop, waiting for your colleagues to hear the news and come gloating. Maybe one of them will be charitable enough to hire you on at their business after you sell your store to pay off the creditors. Maybe you’ll be desperate enough to accept their pity.
