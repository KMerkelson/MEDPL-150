GOOD END 3

When the Syndicate tries to sneak into the Guild’s office, they are met with little resistance. The heist goes off without a hitch, and none of the culprits can be identified. Your fellow craftsmen are devastated.

A package appears on your doorstep. Inside are the siren scales and a note. It thanks you for your good work and commends you for your skill in potion brewing. In fact, it says, the Syndicate might want to call upon your services again. Surely you wouldn’t refuse perfectly good business from paying customers?

You think you’ll be paying the price for their help for a long time yet.
