BAD END 3

When the Syndicate tries to sneak into the Guild’s office, they are met with clouds of powder revealing their forms and globs of slime trapping them in place. The thieves are sent to jail and you are honored as a hero.

Unfortunately, you aren’t able to appreciate any of this. As soon as you told the Guild what you’d done for the Syndicate, you felt a terrible sense of dread. Moments later, Clause 3, Subsection 2 of the magically-enforced contract you didn’t read very well went into effect.

Years later, your colleagues would sometimes bitterly joke that they always did figure you’d die of a heart attack.
