AGREE TO THE DEAL

You wouldn’t be here if you felt you had any other choice. You sign the contract in athame-drawn blood, and it glows with wonderfully ominous red light. Then the bag goes back over your head and you’re taken back to the shop.

It’s no difficulty at all for you to whip up the Elixir of Cloaking. Anyone who assumed this would be your first time clearly never heard about the practical joke wars of your university years. No, what troubles you is your growing certainty that this potion will be indeed used to steal from the Alchemists’ Guild. Suspicious characters lurk around in the shadows around its office, where you know the vault contains irreplaceable artifacts of esteemed alchemists past—and a large sum of the members’ monthly dues. Your guildmates confirm that a master crafter drew the ire of a man rumored to be a Don of the Syndicate. You recall the leering satisfaction on that weaselly criminal’s face as you signed the paper. He must have been delighting in the irony of it all.

As soon as the elixir is ready, burly henchmen come to collect it. You could not bear to sabotage its brewing, too prideful in your work and afraid of detection. But now that your part is done, perhaps they won’t be watching so closely. You might have a chance to alert the Guild before they strike. On the other hand, do you want to be the next one to draw the ire of dangerous criminals? You would have nobody to warn you if they robbed you blind…or worse.
