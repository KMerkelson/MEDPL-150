POTION QUEST

You are an alchemist. No, not just an alchemist—an excellent alchemist. You pride yourself on your encyclopedic knowledge of reagents, your patience with even the most laborious recipes, and your adaptability under pressure.

You have always, always left your clients satisfied, no matter how difficult their requests. And it’s not just the bare minimum, either. Not like those cheap hacks who use the glands of salamanders instead of dragons or water down their concoctions. No, you’ve poured vast amounts of time, effort, and money into making your products top quality.

You have, perhaps, poured a little too much of your own money into your potions. Your business is struggling and the bills are piling up. The other members of the Alchemists' Guild, your colleagues and rivals both, eye your storefront with hunger and pity; you don’t know which one stings worse.

But your business philosophy might be paying off right now, because you’ve attracted the attention of the rich and powerful Count Adesso. The payment for this commission will be enough to pay your debts, and he's even promised in writing to endorse your business. If you complete this job, you’ll be set for life!

There’s just one tiny problem. This request is impossible!

This entitled nitwit is asking you to make him a Draught of Correspondence, a potion that imbues one’s speech with incredible persuasive power. Diplomats use it to arrange delicate treaties, priests to convert the masses to their faith, and aristocrats (like the Count) to rule with silver tongues. Of course, that last capacity is strictly illegal, but legal consequences are easy to avoid for the rich and powerful.

Such powerful magic is unsurprisingly achieved through the application of an equally powerful component: siren scales, ripped from the feet of those elusive and dangerous bird-women. There is absolutely no way that you can afford to purchase the amount you need, and of course your client couldn’t be bothered to supply them for you!

But you have not come this far to let it all come crashing down now. You will find a way to complete this order, at any cost. But how?
