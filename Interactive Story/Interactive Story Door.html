USE THE BACK DOOR

You quietly slip through the back door. Tip-toeing through the shelves, you squint at the labels printed on the crates. Harpy feathers, elephant tusks, powdered spices, Hesperidean apples…then, at long last, you spot what you’re looking for. Carefully, you pull a flask of acid from your bag and melt the lock. You pull open the lid—

Oh god, why is it so creaky?

You scoop a bunch of scales into your bag, close the lid, and turn around to see a fresh-faced guard staring at you in shock.

Quickly—what do you do?
